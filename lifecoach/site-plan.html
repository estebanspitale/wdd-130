Home page

Our goal is your success. We offer one-on-one, group and entrepreneurial coaching programs to help people realize their potential and live their lives more fully. Work connects people with themselves so they can understand what's important to them, investigate the resources available to them in the environments in which they thrive, and make powerful and resonant decisions to build a life of greater happiness and success by achieving their personal, group, and work goals. The people we work with are already successful. They are creative, challenging, and ambitious. They only come to us to deepen and expand their thinking and explore more than they are capable of. This work invites you to see yourself more fully, evaluate what old options are no longer useful and what new options will serve you to move forward on this path that we are all traveling.

LifeCoaching

Life coaching is one of the most widespread coaching modalities. Its object of study is the person as such, their emotions, and feelings, and as in the other branches, it proposes a dialectical relationship between the coach and the coachee based on feedback. A coach is not only useful when facing personal or emotional problems. A coach is above all a facilitator who can be present in family, affective, professional, or work processes. So that you can better understand, here are some common situations in which a person could benefit from a life coach: • When starting a new project: You would be surprised to know the large number of entrepreneurs who seek support and advice before launching a new company. • When we face situations of change: Many people do not know how to deal with difficult situations such as job loss, divorce, or bereavement. • Search for direction in professional goals: Life coaching also helps professionals clarify their goals and carry out concrete actions to achieve them. • Lack of motivation: People do not always have enough motivation to follow their goals. Motivational life coaching seeks to restore confidence and security to each person, two key elements in any process. • Promoting new talents: Many people have a specific skill that they don't know how to carry out. A life coach helps facilitate those resources and thus bring out other skills. • When we want to eradicate destructive patterns: Negativity, pessimism, lack of interest, fear of change and inflexibility are aspects that prevent the achievement of objectives. The role of a coach is to eradicate these barriers and make the coachee aware of the vitality of reversing these thoughts. All these reasons are enough for you to use life coaching as a support tool. The essence of these practices is the presence of an external gaze that helps you get answers about your own life.

Sessions

If you want to elevate your goals, achieve your goals, and broaden your horizons by moving toward a fuller, more fulfilling life, we can begin working together with an initial phone contact who will have 30 minutes to clarify what to expect, talk about your goals, answer your questions, and experience a short training session. Only if after this call you feel at ease, we can work together constructively and establish a new commitment. Life coaching sessions can be on Zoom, Teams, some other platform, over the phone, or in person, depending on what you feel can serve you best and fits other existing commitments. Tasks and consultations will be carried out that are meant to deepen the new awareness gained in the sessions and establish positive habits as you move from where you were to where you want to be. The duration of the process is subject to the evolution that we have in each session. Throughout the training program, cross-session support is offered with short check-ins, emails, and messages. My goal is to keep the space where you can safely explore how you want to be in the world and what factors, both internal and external, are preventing it. Contact: Phone, Email, Social networks
