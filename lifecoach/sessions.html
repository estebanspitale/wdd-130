Home LifeCoaching Sessions

If you want to elevate your goals, achieve your goals, and broaden your horizons by moving toward a fuller, more fulfilling life, we can begin working together with an initial phone contact who will have 30 minutes to clarify what to expect, talk about your goals, answer your questions, and experience a short training session. Only if after this call you feel at ease, we can work together constructively and establish a new commitment. Life coaching sessions can be on Zoom, Teams, some other platform, over the phone, or in person, depending on what you feel can serve you best and fits other existing commitments. Tasks and consultations will be carried out that are meant to deepen the new awareness gained in the sessions and establish positive habits as you move from where you were to where you want to be. The duration of the process is subject to the evolution that we have in each session. Throughout the training program, cross-session support is offered with short check-ins, emails, and messages. My goal is to keep the space where you can safely explore how you want to be in the world and what factors, both internal and external, are preventing it.
